How BMI Works

BMI is calculated using weight (kg) divided by height squared (m²). It helps determine whether your weight is healthy for your height.

BMI Categories

Underweight
< 18.5
Normal
18.5 – 24.9
Overweight
25 – 29.9
Obese
30+

Health Guidance

If You Are Underweight

Focus on nutrient-rich foods and strength training exercises.

If You Are Overweight

Try regular physical activity and a balanced diet.

If You Are Obese

Consult a healthcare professional and follow a structured plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BMI accurate?

BMI is a general guide and may not reflect muscle mass.

What is a healthy BMI?

Between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.
